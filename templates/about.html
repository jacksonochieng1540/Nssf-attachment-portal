About NSSF Attachment Portal

Streamlining Student Industrial Attachments

The NSSF Attachment Portal is a comprehensive system designed to facilitate the management of student industrial attachments while ensuring compliance with National Social Security Fund (NSSF) regulations.

Our Mission

To provide an efficient, transparent, and user-friendly platform that connects students, companies, and educational institutions while ensuring full compliance with NSSF requirements throughout the attachment period.

Key Features

Student Management

Complete profile management and attachment registration for students.

Company Portal

Dedicated interface for companies to manage students and submit NSSF returns.

NSSF Compliance

Automated tracking and reporting of NSSF requirements for all attachments.

Reporting System

Comprehensive reporting tools for administrators to monitor the attachment program.
